Spinners

GhostCSS has great looking spinners that can be customized. Let's take a look at them:

<div class="spinner"></div>
<div class="spinner text-green-800"></div>
<div class="spinner text-gray-600"></div>
<div class="spinner text-purple-900"></div>

Grow

GhostCSS has another animation for spinners:

<div class="spinner-grow bg-red-500 border-none"></div>
<div class="spinner-grow bg-green-800 border-none"></div>
<div class="spinner-grow bg-gray-600 border-none"></div>
