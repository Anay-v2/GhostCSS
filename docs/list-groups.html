List Groups

GhostCSS has great looking list groups that can be customized. Let's take a look at them:

  • foo
  • bar
  • baz
<ul class="list-group">
<li class="list-group-item">foo</li>
<li class="list-group-item">bar</li>
<li class="list-group-item">baz</li>
</ul>
