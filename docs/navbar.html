Navbar

GhostCSS provides a good design for navbars. Lets look at it:

Brand

  • foo
  • bar
  • baz
<div class="navbar bg-green-500">
<h1 class="navbar-brand">Brand</h1>
<ul class="navbar-content !text-gray-400">
<li class="nav-link !hover:text-white"><a href="#" class="text-black">foo</a></li>
<li class="nav-link !hover:text-white"><a href="#">bar</a></li>
<li class="nav-link !hover:text-white"><a href="#">baz</a></li>
</ul>
</div>

Navbar text

GhostCSS has styles for navbar text. Let's look at them:

Brand

  • foo
  • bar
  • baz
lorem
<div class="navbar bg-green-500">
<h1 class="navbar-brand">Brand</h1>
<ul class="navbar-content !text-gray-400">
<li class="nav-link !hover:text-white"><a href="#" class="text-black">foo</a></li>
<li class="nav-link !hover:text-white"><a href="#">bar</a></li>
<li class="nav-link !hover:text-white"><a href="#">baz</a></li>
</ul>
<span class="navbar-text">lorem</span>
</div>

</ul>

</div>
