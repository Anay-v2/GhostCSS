GhostCSS

Build responsive and good-looking sites without ever leaving your HTML with GhostCSS, the next generation of css.

<div class="flex sm:block">
  <h1 class="text-4xl sm:text-2xl">Responsive layouts are so easy!</h1>

It's responsive

Worried about mobiles? Just chuck in an sm: or lg: behind any class and we will take care of it.

Great Color Pallete

GhostCSS has a great color pallete. even with the same layout you can add different colors and make it look better.

<div class="bg-purple-400">
  <h1 class="text-4xl text-green-500">Colors are cool!</h1>
  <p class="text-green-300">lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipisicing elit</p>
</div>
<div class="bg-purple-400">
  <h1 class="bg-green-500 hover:bg-green-700">States!</h1>
  <p class="text-green-300">lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipisicing elit <a class="text-yellow-800 hover:text-yellow-900 hover:translate-y-8">lorem</a> </p>
</div>

We got states

Want to style something on focus or hover? just stick hover: or focus: behind any class and watch the magic happen. We even have custom states like group-hover and stuff like empty and marker.

Dark Mode

Don't want to blind your users when they open your website at 3AM? Enable dark mode and add dark: behind any class and solve the problem!

<div class="bg-gray-400 dark:bg-gray-800">
  <h1 class="text-black dark:text-white">Dark mode!</h1>
  <p class="text-gray-200 dark:text-gray-600">lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipisicing elit</p>
</div>
<div class="card">
  <button class="btn btn-outline-red">Components!</h1>

</div>

Components

Want to avoid the hard work and get good looking components? Just add classes like btn and navbar to anything and we will style it for you!
